The Difference

Three things this is not.

i.

Curated, never browsed

A small number of thoughtfully chosen introductions arrive each week — not an endless feed. Read deeply, choose slowly. There is no clock here.

ii.

Faith-centered, in practice

Profiles include the season you’re walking through, the verse you live by, and what faith actually looks like in your life — not just church affiliation.

iii.

Community before chemistry

Faith Circles bring 8–16 people together for four weeks of moderated conversation. Authentic personlities are discovered over time in a group setting, not in a single profile.

The Process

Four steps. No swiping.

  1. one

    Reflect

    Six short reflection questions, no filter sliders. Your season, your rhythm, what you wrestle with.

  2. two

    Be introduced

    A human matchmaker reviews each pairing. A few each week, with a short note explaining why.

  3. three

    Begin

    When both say yes, conversation opens. No timers, no streaks, no double-blue-tick anxiety.

  4. four

    Belong

    Join a Faith Circle of 8–16 like-minded people. Four weeks of moderated conversation. Sometimes a one-to-one comes from it.
